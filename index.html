Who Are We

We are students from Borough of Manhattan Community College who want to make an impact by building a platform to help you better understand this global crises.

We will not only provide you some useful resources but also give you a chance to contribute. This site is useful for students or anyone who wants to expand their knowledge on water scarcity and its causes.
