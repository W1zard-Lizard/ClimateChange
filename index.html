Who Are We

We are students from Borough of Manhattan Community College and our goal is to spread awareness on the importance of water sustainability.

We will not only provide you some useful resources but also give you a chance to contribute. This site is useful for students or anyone who wants to expand their knowledge on water scarcity and its causes. You can also contribute to this site by sharing any relevant ideas, articles or resouces.We would be happy to add your work to our site and make sure you get credit for it.
